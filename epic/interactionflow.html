Game and Fish Registration

Title: Applying for hunts and downloading content

Name of user and their role:

John Montgomery is a new user and needs to enroll for hunts and download the most up to date rules and regulations.

Usage preconditions:

Must have a personal account and have payment information on file to apply

Usage postconditons:

Registering allows him to apply for hunts and download other content to his devices.

Interaction flow: Create an account for NM Game and Fish

  • Mr. Montgomery enters the http://www.wildlife.state.nm.us website into his Iphone browser.
  • The NM Game and Fish website pops up on his browser
  • John Selects the Customer Login option on the top of the page.
  • John Selects 'Create a New Account'
  • NM Game and fish directs John to Read and agree to the terms and conditions, before he continues.
  • After accepting, the page directs John to fill in all his personal information, name, DOB, create a password and enter an email address.
  • He clicks continue, and the next page has John fill in his additional contact informatio. (ex:ph:, address and email confirmation.
  • John clicks next page and the page has John review his information, beore he hit's complete.
  • John is directed back to the customer login page, where he will now use his username and password to login.
  • John logs in and now can select "Draw Hunt Applications" on the left tool bar.
  • John review's his hunts and is directed to the 'Checkout Cart'
  • The page directs John to the payment screen, where he must enter his credit card information
  • John clicks purchase and the Game and Fish website send's John an e-mail confirmation for his payment
  • John is now a user for Game and Fish and can view all his purchases and not have to wait on the phone for hours.

    • Frequency of Use:
  • 4-5 times a week
