How To register for hunters

Title: Downloading the proclamation and applying for hunts.

Name of user and their role:

John Montgomery is a new user and needs to enroll for hunts and download a proclamation (hunting rules).

Usage preconditions:

Must have a valid account under his name and personal information. Must enter payment information at end of application

Usage postconditons:

Registering for an account allows you to apply for hunt lottery and see results.

Interaction flow:
  • Mr. Montgomery enters the http://www.wildlife.state.nm.us website into his Iphone browser.
  • Select the Customer Login option on the top of the page.
  • John Selects 'Create a New Account'
  • He will then have to Read and agree to the terms and conditions
  • John will fill in all his personal information, name, DOB, create a password and enter an email address.
  • The next screen will ask for his contact information. (ex:ph:, address and email confirmation.
  • He willnow review his information on the 'Final Review' page and select finish.
  • John will now recieve a confirmation e-mail from NM Game and Fish and other updates through the year.
  • Now that he has created an account he can select "Draw Hunt Applications" to apply for hunts
  • After reviewing his hunts he can now enter in his credit care information for payment
  • To confirm that he has applied, he may now click on "My Purchases" which will alow him to view the status and if he was selected to hunt
  • Once the lottery has been completed in late April, he can view my "Purchases" or recieve an e-mail confirmation that he was selected.
  • John can visit this site to view updates across the State for hunting, fishing, new rules, regulations and no longer need to carry a paper proclamation, as he can download and save this to his phone. .
